Questions I get asked a lot

Do you charge per hour or per project?

Normally, I charge per project. I've learnt that clients prefer that I fix a price rather than count hours.

You're in a vastly different timezone to us, is that ok?

Yup! In recent years I've become accustomed to working with clients from a variety of different timezones simultaneously.

What payment methods do you accept?

Bank transfer seems to be the easiest. I have a bank account both in the UK & Hong Kong and can provide an International Bank Acount Number (IBAN) & SWIFT/BIC codes for each. Cheques are also ok!

So, how much do you charge?

It completely depends on what you want me to do, my availability, and how quickly you you need me to get started. Let's chat and take things from there.

Will we sign an agreement/contract for the project terms?

Yes. Although I've never had a dispute with a client over a project, I feel it's better to be safe than sorry, for both parties.
